Quick Start

  1. Open Mimic Studio and start a new idea or project.
  2. Allow microphone access when you want to record audio.
  3. Record a short vocal, humming, rhythm, or sound idea.
  4. Preview your audio, refine your session, and export when ready.

Microphone Permission

If recording does not start, confirm that microphone access is enabled in your device settings. Mimic Studio uses the microphone to record humming, vocals, and audio ideas.

Audio Not Playing

  • Raise your device volume and check silent mode or connected headphones.
  • Close and reopen the app if audio playback appears stuck.
  • Try recording a short new sample to confirm the audio session is working.

Export or Sharing Issues

  • Make sure the project has audio content before exporting.
  • Confirm there is enough available device storage.
  • Retry export after closing other heavy apps if your device is low on memory.

Report a Problem

Email cserrano85@gmail.com with a short description of the issue, your device model, app version, and the steps that caused the problem.

For the fastest help, include what you were trying to do: record, import, preview, transform, save, or export.
